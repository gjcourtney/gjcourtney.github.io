Risk & Regret

Men at Trent Vineyard

Each event is designed to be a shallow dive into spiritual formation - the life-long journey of becoming more like Christ for the sake of others. Each event is a starting point, but we would encourage you to keep thinking and processing what you've heard, asking the Holy Spirit what He is highlighting to you and what your next step in this journey should be.

Poem

Why are adults always in a rush?

There is no place to settle.
Resembling a ravine,
Just like a wondering thought (...)
It keeps going until it gets caught.
But, by that time it’s too late,
The action is unstoppable,

It’s too fierce to stop.
Then looking behind; there’s hindsight, I can feel its burning eye sight,
Telling me “The outcome is not what you expected,”

That which was once carefully protected.
Now neglected.
Shattered dreams is all we’re left with.

Regret

Regret starts defacing the words of life.
Heavy hearts absorb disappointment
The shrill wave of deferred hope
Everything it says makes sense,
We let regret set in,
We let regret stay,
We let regret have its way.

There was one time...I felt empowered to speak my mind
To a room full of those I believed to be aligned.
I talked a vision that was whispered to me
In intimacy
And I revealed it all, so cheaply to see.
Like Joseph in his dreams
The ravine swept in
Now isolated I begin.

I acted too hastily
I thought I heard him right!
I couldn’t wait for God to show up,
He takes ages!
But ... maybe I missed his timing.
I regret not trusting God.

Standing in shame, through the fear of man,
He comes running as fast He can
To tell the truth
Words that will change your life!
He says, “Where are your accusers now?”

Jesus breaks the tall walls of shame
Freedom floods the inner courts
The drench of grace covers all.

Enter the throne room.
In His presence, I change my mind
He doesn’t fold his arms
He doesn’t say “I told you so”,

With loving kindness, alone in his presence
He waits for me
He actually listens
To me.

You see, regrets can be dealt with
Take a risk for God. Trust,
Place your heart on that unseen.

Cast all your cares, leave it all behind
Look not to the left or the right
Only at His eyes.

Every attempt to bring you down
Gets reversed by the one who wears the crown.
In this freedom; Rest and keep resting
Stand and keep standing
Walk and keep walking
Run and keep running
Regrets can lead to wisdom, risk is a part of learning
He will redeem it all.
Free people are dangerous.
